TASK 2 - COMPARING GESTURE RECOGNIZERS
======================================

Builds a custom test dataset of unistroke gestures, trains five LSTM
classifiers of decreasing size on Wobbrock et al.'s unistroke gesture
logs, and compares their parameter count, accuracy, and prediction time
against my own captured test data. Results and discussion are reported
in unistroke_gestures.ipynb, as required by the assignment.

FILES
=====

capture_logs.py
----------------
Pyglet script used to capture my own gesture samples for the test set

unistroke_gestures.ipynb
-------------------------
Main notebook for this task.

IMPORTANT: My own gesture sample logs are stored in the datasets folder (sibling folder of task_2).

NOTES
=====
- Wobbrock's logs dataset includes "question_mark" as one of its 16
  gesture classes, while the $1 recognizer's templates (task_1/
  recognizer.py) use "zig_zag" instead. question_mark samples were
  still captured and used to train the LSTM, but this class will need
  to be excluded if the LSTM is ever compared against the $1 recognizer.
- capture_logs.py was written for personal, one-off use during data
  capture. It lacks features like redoing a specific gesture or
  discarding a bad sample mid-session, since that wasn't required by
  the assignment.
- The comparison between the LSTM models and the $1 gesture recognizer
  (also required by the assignment) is missing due to lack of time.
