TASK 3 - GESTURE DETECTION GAME
===============================

"Ghost Buster": a small pyglet game controlled with three unistroke
gestures (rectangle, pigtail, caret), built on top of task_1's $1
recognizer, with some of the game-state logic reused from Assignment
01 ("Fishy").

FILES
=====

gesture_application.py
------------------------
The game itself. Reuses the DollarRecognizer and Point classes from
task_1/recognizer.py. Ghosts of three types (rectangle, pigtail,
caret) spawn on the right side of the screen and drift left; drawing
the matching gesture inside a fixed drawing zone banishes the closest
ghost of that type and adds a point. Letting a ghost cross the red
"danger line" near the left edge costs one of three lives; losing all
lives ends the game and shows the final score.
